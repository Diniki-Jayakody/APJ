Questions Students Ask

FAQ content makes the website more professional and reduces repeated WhatsApp questions.

Can APJ guarantee visa approval?

No consultancy can guarantee an embassy decision. APJ can guide students professionally and prepare a complete visa file.

Can I select a country after consultation?

Yes. APJ can compare countries based on education level, financial ability, future goals and course availability.

Can APJ support accommodation and ticketing?

Yes. After visa approval, APJ can guide students with accommodation options, travel insurance, ticketing and pre-departure preparation.
